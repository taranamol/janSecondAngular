weather in amritsar, panjab during winter:

Winter lasts from November to March with temperature ranges from 4 °C to about 19 °C. During this season, Amritsar is affected by cold waves and the minimum temperature drops down to a degree or two below the freezing point of water. Frosts are common during the cold season. Please prepare accordingly!
